Eric Ellis is a graphic designer based in Charleston South Carolina and a senior designer at Fuzzco. I enjoy long walks on the beach and inspirational TED talks. I used to live in Chicago but that was then, this is now. When not graphic designing I may be found staring blankly into space, vacuuming a floor, reading a book, occasionally traveling, skateboarding or antiquing with Frances. Unless otherwise noted, all work shown above completed with Fuzzco.

Charleston, SC 29403
info@ericellis.net



Formerly

I graduated with a BFA from Columbia College Chicago in 2010 and started my career at Ogilvy, as part of their internal brand team, 485, led by Gabe Usadel. I was part of several large campaigns, most noteably the relaunch of the UPS brand. After three years to the day at O. I decamped to a small scrappy digital agency called Nelson Cash, where I spent two years as a designer, working primarily with Google, but also on a lot of other digital work. In August 2015, after 11 years in Chicago and two years at NC, I accepted a position as senior designer at Fuzzco and so Frances and I packed our life into the back of a rented truck and drove south to live the salt life.
