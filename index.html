The Gist
Eric Ellis is a graphic designer at Fuzzco in Charleston, South Carolina, United States, Earth. Graduate of Columbia College Chicago, he holds a BFA with a focus in graphic design. Since 2015 he has worked alongside his colleagues on a wide range of projects, for Dropbox, Rippling, Google, URBN, East Fork, Obey and many others. Previously he held positions at Ogilvy & Mather and Nelson Cash in Chicago.
