The Gist
Eric Ellis (b. 1986) is a graphic designer at Fuzzco in Charleston, South Carolina, United States, Earth. Graduate of Columbia College Chicago, he holds a BFA with a focus in graphic design. Since 2015 he has worked alongside his colleagues at Fuzzco on a wide range of projects, for Figma, Notion, First Round Ventures, Dropbox, Google, URBN, Obey and many others. Previously he held positions at Ogilvy & Mather and Nelson Cash in Chicago.
